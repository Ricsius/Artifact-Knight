Basic instructions

The story

A professor who studies magical artifacts, asked you to bring him some lost artfacts.

You are shielded from the harmful curses, however the professor is not, so choose your items wisely.

Contorls

  • Escape: Pauses the game.
  • Up arrow: Jump.
  • Down arrow: Pick up artifacts / show your artifacts to the professor / open doors.
  • Left arrow: Move your character to to left.
  • Right arrow: Move your character to to right.
  • I button: Opens your inventory.
